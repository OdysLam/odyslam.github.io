About me

Hey,

My name is Odysseas, and I study electrical engineering in the University of Patras,Greece, currently in the second year. I try to be as active as possible both in the student community and in my personal time, which I spend doing personal projects or promoting entrepreneurship in Patras.Ooh, I forgot, I am deeply in love with entrepreneurship, with being active and creative in any way possible.I believe that being productive can deeply change a person from within, having experienced it, I try to make the student community to be productive and engage in extra-curiculum activities that are realated to the field of their particular studies.

I chose to study electrical engineering because I was always keen in technology and electronics.Moreover, the more I researched the area, the more I was fascinated by the nearly endless possibilities of the field,giving me more than a handful of ideas of what to pursue later. Mostly in robotics,nanotechnology and microprocessors and generally all things small.

Lastly I am also very interested in public relations and marketing, as I constantly try to hone my people and communication skills.I firmly believe that social skills provide solid foundations and are critical in success, no matter the field.
