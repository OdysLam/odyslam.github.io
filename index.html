The Project

The Idea

All started when I wanted to start exploring the world of electronics, which afterall is one of the subjects of my studies. Moreoever I wanted to start a usable project, something that would not only be educative, but also usefull . I also found IoT quite fancinating, something that is going to dominate the technology market in the next few decafed allong with-or in conjuction with - VR.

I was starting from absolute 0, so Arduino was the obvious choise because of it’s easy and straight-forward. The project did not evolve much as Arduino is fairly limited, especially if you want web capabilities.

At this point, Raspberry creeped in and suddenly everything turned green and shiny. It was able to do what I wanted and the best part? it’s codable in python, one of the easiest and most versatile programming languages I know.

Project?

As I said it’s IoT related and more specifically in the area of smart-home. I have automated some tasks, like locking and unlocking my door, and I plan to add many features, all controlled via a Web Interface.I plan to explaing everything I have done in detail, and I hope that it serves as an easy guide for starters in order to dwelve into both the world of IoT and the world of Raspberry.

So..now what?

The project is currently frozen at ver 0.1 as I want to bring the blog up to date and then continue with it. At the next blog I will briefly explain raspberry pi, just to make sure we are at the same page.

Until then, cheers!
