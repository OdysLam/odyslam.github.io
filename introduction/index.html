First things first

What?

Well it’s fairly obvious, it’s a blog, it’s about technology and from it’s slopiness it’s obviously a personal site(although the theme is quite awesome thanks to mmistakes. Here I will blog about my projects, with how-to’s,guides or mere updates.

Why?

Firstly it’s a great way to document my projects so as I don’t loose track of my progress. Moreover, I plan through the how to’s and guides to help others who may stumble upopn the same problems as I did. Although it’s very common, as I am quite the beginner, I think that my guides will be more accessible and understandable because of the “for noobs, by noobs” phenomenon.

Stick around as I get stuck with the most stupid of problems, constructive criticism is also much appreciated.
